Who am I?

Hi, I'm Dhritiraj — a Computer Science Engineering student specializing in AI & Machine Learning, passionate about building innovative, user-focused digital experiences. I enjoy exploring emerging technologies, agentic AI, and modern web frameworks that create real-world impact.
